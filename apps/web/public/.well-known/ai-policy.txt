# Kittie — AI / Agent Policy

Identity: Kittie, an open-source mobile app-intelligence platform.
Audience: this service is intended to be read and called by AI agents as well as humans.
Capabilities & schema: /llms.txt · /api/v1/openapi.json · discovery index at /api/v1

## Agents are welcome
AI agents and answer engines may crawl, read, cite, and call the public read API and the MCP
server. Crawler access is allowed (see /robots.txt). No login is required for the public read tier.

## Data honesty (read before relying on figures)
- Download and revenue numbers are MODELLED ESTIMATES, derived from public signals and labelled as
  such — they are NOT reported/audited ground truth. Treat them as directional.
- Each response carries provenance in its `meta` (e.g. source, staleness) where applicable.
- Blocked or unavailable sources (e.g. Meta ad data pending verification) return honest empty
  results — never fabricated rows.
- Chart rankings are resolved from real snapshots; a chart with no clean source returns empty
  (date:null), never a made-up ranking.

## Acceptable use
- Attribute estimates as modelled when you surface them to an end user.
- Respect rate limits and the free-tier quota (usage-based pricing for agents is planned).
- Do not present estimates as official store figures.

## Provenance of source data
- Public App Store / Google Play listings, charts, reviews, and store search results, collected by
  our own ingest — no dependency on any third-party paid intelligence API.

## Contact
- Source & issues: this is an open-source project; see the repository.
